01 — ABOUT SHILTECH

Engineering discipline, applied consistently

SHILTECH Engineering Services was built to give Nigerian industry a single, accountable engineering partner — from first site visit to final commissioning.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

A multidisciplinary engineering firm, built around delivery

SHILTECH Engineering Services provides electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, procurement, and industrial maintenance to manufacturing plants, oil & gas operators, warehouses, government agencies, and commercial facilities across Nigeria. We combine technical certification with hands-on execution — our engineers design, fabricate, install, and commission, and stay on for the maintenance support after handover.

Every scope, whatever the size, runs through the same eight-stage engineering process and the same HSE standard.

HISTORY

Where we started

Founded to close a gap in the Nigerian market for an engineering partner that could handle electrical, mechanical, and procurement work under one accountable roof — instead of clients coordinating three separate vendors.

VISION

Where we're going

To be recognised as one of Nigeria's leading engineering and industrial solutions companies — trusted for technical expertise, reliability, and safety on every site we touch.

MISSION

How we get there

Deliver innovative mechanical, electrical, procurement, and industrial engineering solutions with uncompromising quality, safety, and accountability — on schedule, every time.

LEADERSHIP

Founder & Lead Engineer

SHILTECH is led from the front — our founder remains hands-on across major engineering scopes, site assessments, and client relationships. That direct involvement is a deliberate choice: it keeps design decisions accountable to the person whose name is on the company, and it keeps SHILTECH close to the realities of every site.

Under this leadership, SHILTECH has built a team of certified engineers across electrical, mechanical, and procurement disciplines, united by the same standard: engineer it right, deliver it safely.

ENGINEERING PHILOSOPHY

Specification before speed

We treat the design and assessment phases as the highest-leverage part of any project — a scope that's engineered correctly the first time avoids the rework, downtime, and safety exposure that come from rushing to site. Every design is reviewed against the applicable standard before proposal, and every installation is quality-inspected before testing and commissioning sign-off.
